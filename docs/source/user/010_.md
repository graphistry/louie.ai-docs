# Interface Navigation

## Connector Status & Organization Context

![Interface Navigation](./images/user/010_thread_screenshot.png)

### Organization Context

You login under an organization context, shown by the profile area in the interface. You may belong to several organizations:
- Your unaffiliated personal account
- Your team
- Teams you collaborate with
- And other organizational contexts

### Thread Organization

Within the active organization, you can create threads in two locations:
- Your personal area (private threads)
- The organization's shared area (team threads)

### Data Connectors

Your organization has live data connectors that provide access to various data sources:
- Green indicators show healthy connections
- You can ask Louie to re-index connectors when needed
- Connectors provide seamless access to your data sources

![Connector Status](./images/user/010__1.png)

## Navigation Tips

- Use the organization switcher to move between different organizational contexts
- Create new threads using the "+" button
- Access your personal and shared threads from the main navigation
- Monitor connector health through the status indicators
- Use the profile menu to access settings and account options
